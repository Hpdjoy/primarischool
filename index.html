About Us

P.S. Shahri, established in 1964, is managed by the Department of Education. Located in a rural area within the Karakat block of the Rohtas district, Bihar, the school serves students from Grades 1 to 5. It is a co-educational institution and does not have a pre-primary section. The school operates as a single-session institution. Hindi is the medium of instruction. Accessible by an all-weather road, the school ensures ease of approachability, and its academic session begins in April each year.
